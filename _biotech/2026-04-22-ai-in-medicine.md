## Introduction

Artificial intelligence is reshaping how medicine is practiced, researched, and
regulated. This entry outlines key themes for this journal's coverage of AI in
medicine — reliability, data reusability, and translational impact.

## Themes

- **Model reliability**: benchmarking clinical ML models on real-world,
  out-of-distribution cohorts.
- **Data reusability**: FAIR data practices for imaging, EHR, and -omics.
- **Clinical translation**: moving from retrospective validation to prospective
  deployment and post-market surveillance.
- **Regulation and governance**: FDA, EMA, and institutional oversight of
  adaptive algorithms.
