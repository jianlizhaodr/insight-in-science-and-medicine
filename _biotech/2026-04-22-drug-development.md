## Introduction

Drug development translates biological insight into approved therapies. This
entry frames the journal's coverage of the full pipeline — from target
discovery through post-market surveillance — with an emphasis on reliability
and data reusability.

## Pipeline stages

- **Target discovery and validation**: genetic, chemical, and functional
  evidence for causal disease drivers.
- **Lead identification and optimization**: high-throughput screening, DNA-encoded
  libraries, structure- and AI-guided design.
- **Preclinical pharmacology**: ADME, toxicology, PK/PD modeling,
  translational biomarkers.
- **Clinical development**: Phase I–III design, adaptive trials, master
  protocols, endpoint selection.
- **Regulatory and post-market**: FDA/EMA review, REMS, real-world evidence,
  post-approval safety.

## Methodological focus

- **Model-informed drug development (MIDD)**: PBPK, population PK,
  disease-progression models.
- **AI/ML in discovery**: generative chemistry, structure prediction, target
  deconvolution.
- **Trial efficiency**: synthetic control arms, *in silico* patient cohorts,
  decentralized trial designs.
- **Data reusability**: FAIR clinical and preclinical datasets; linked
  outcomes for meta-analysis.
