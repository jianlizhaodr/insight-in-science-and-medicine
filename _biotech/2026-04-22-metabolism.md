## Introduction

Metabolism underlies health and disease at every scale, from single-cell
bioenergetics to whole-organism physiology. This entry frames the journal's
coverage of metabolic research and its translational applications.

## Themes

- **Cellular bioenergetics**: mitochondrial function, redox balance, nutrient
  sensing pathways (mTOR, AMPK).
- **Systems metabolism**: inter-organ crosstalk, circadian regulation,
  metabolic flexibility.
- **Metabolomics**: mass-spec and NMR profiling, flux analysis, stable-isotope
  tracing.
- **Disease-linked metabolism**: diabetes, NAFLD/MASLD, cancer metabolism,
  neurodegeneration.
- **Therapeutics**: GLP-1 analogs, SGLT2 inhibitors, ketogenic interventions,
  metabolic reprogramming in oncology.

## Data and methods

- Multi-modal datasets: metabolomics paired with transcriptomics and clinical
  phenotypes
- Computational models: genome-scale metabolic reconstructions,
  constraint-based flux analysis
- Translational endpoints: glucose dynamics, lipid panels, body composition,
  hard clinical outcomes
